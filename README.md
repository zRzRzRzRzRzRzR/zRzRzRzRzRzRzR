# Hey 👋, I'm zR

<img align="right" src="https://github.com/zRzRzRzRzRzRzR/zRzRzRzRzRzRzR/blob/main/Pic/logo.png" width="260"/>

I am currently working at [Z.ai](http://z.ai). In parallel, I am pursuing my PhD at the [🇬🇧 University of Liverpool](https://www.liverpool.ac.uk) and visiting [🇦🇪 Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence](https://mbzuai.ac.ae) as a Visiting PhD. 

I am passionate about open-source initiatives and hope to keep exploring this field in depth. My main experiences include the following:

+ 📄 **Open-source model development as part of my work**  
  I work on bridging model research and real-world engineering, contributing to open-source models such as [GLM-5.2](https://arxiv.org/abs/2602.15763), [GLM-V](https://arxiv.org/abs/2507.01006), [CogVideoX](https://arxiv.org/abs/2408.06072), and [CogAgent](https://arxiv.org/abs/2312.08914), as well as practical engineering systems such as [Open-AutoGLM](https://github.com/zai-org/Open-AutoGLM).

+ ⭐ **Open-source projects I was previously involved in**  
  I have also participated in open-source projects related to model agents and agent frameworks, such as [langchain-chatchat](https://github.com/chatchat-space/Langchain-Chatchat) and [chatpdf](https://github.com/CosmosShadow/gptpdf). Although I am no longer part of these project teams, I sincerely hope they continue to grow and benefit the community.

+ 🏆 **Competitions I joined as a student**  
  I previously took part in several national competitions, including [RoboMaster](https://www.robomaster.com/en-US) and the [National Students' SmartCar Competition](https://www.smartcar.zone), where I achieved national-level awards. These competitions were truly inspiring, and I hope they continue to help generations of students learn, build, and improve.
